New Album Release
《没语季节》
01   没语季节
02   盲选
03   没了我你依然拥有太阳
You can click here to view videos related to the album.

Clouds drift in the sky, yet originate from falling rain and flowing rivers. Following the guidance of dreams, wandering with the wind, blossoming all the way, blooming in a broader and more brilliant universe.

A cloud, passing through ten destinations, is a journey worth singing about. This album marks a significant transformation for "Yun" — from "singer" to "musician," from "producer" to "executive producer." Every step was personally undertaken, a testament to her beloved music career and a reflection of her inner journey.

She, amidst the damp and gloomy plum rains, in the fog of lies, in silent gazes, after memories turn to bubbles one by one, in the midst of storms, after the clamor fades to gentle breezes and clear skies, on unknown journeys, in the poet's tone, in the tempered future, on the boundless wilderness — always like a cloud, passing through time and the world.

Once she takes flight, she will never disperse.

Descending, ascending, drifting, surging.

Her voice is clear and true, never forgetting her original aspirations.

Let us look forward to the surprises this "cloud" will bring us in the future.

雲，飘在天空，却始于降落的雨与奔流的河。循理想指引，跟随风流浪，一路生花，盛开在更旷远的灿烂宇宙。

一朵雲，经过十首目的地，是值得被歌唱的成长。这张专辑是“雲”的一次重要蜕变，从“歌手”到“音乐人”，从“制作人”到“出品人”，每一个环节都亲力亲为，是为热爱的音乐事业交出的答卷，也是心路历程的写照。

她，在潮湿阴郁的梅雨中，在迷雾般的谎言里，在沉默的对视中，在回忆一一成为泡影后，在疾风骤雨间，在喧嚣退去的风轻天晴后，在未可知的旅途中，在诗人的口吻里，在淬炼的未来里，在无垠的旷野上，始终如雲，路过时间与世界。

她，一经飞起，便不会散去。

或降落，或升腾，或漂流，或翻涌。

歌声嘹原，不忘初心。

让我们一起期待这朵“云”，带给我们更惊喜的未来。
